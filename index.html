Railway Regency: An Android Application

An interactive mobile android application that enlists the trains by taking source station, destination station, date, and quota.Based on this information the available trains for the route will be displayed along with the fare and the days of the week it runs.Our focus is on minimizing the time an end-user has to wait so as to get ticket availability from a station with minimum waiting or with the desired fare.

Following are the functionalities of App:
✔ Users can set a reminder for the source-destination station on a particular train
and they will be notified when less than 5 tickets are available for that train.
✔ Live Trains status
✔PNR Status
